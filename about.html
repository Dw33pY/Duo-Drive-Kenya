About Duo Drive Kenya

Trusted car dealership dedicated to providing reliable, affordable, and well-inspected vehicles.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Duo Drive Kenya has grown from a small family business to one of Nairobi's most trusted car dealerships. Our journey began with a simple mission: to make quality vehicles accessible to Kenyans at fair prices.

Over the years, we've built a reputation for transparency, reliability, and exceptional customer service. We believe that buying a car should be an exciting experience, not a stressful one.

Our Mission

To provide quality, inspected vehicles with transparent pricing and exceptional customer service to every Kenyan.

Our Vision

To become Kenya's most trusted automotive partner, transforming how people buy and own vehicles.

Our Values

Integrity, Transparency, Quality, and Customer Satisfaction drive everything we do.
